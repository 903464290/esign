Few Steps For a Signature

Scroll Down

  • 01. Registration On The Platform

    To register for our account, enter your account number, password, repeat password and email address.

  • 02. Passing Verification

    Once you have registered you will receive an activation link in your email inbox, click on it to complete your registration.

  • 03. Creating An Application For a Contract

    Click on the signature button on the home page and you will be taken to the contract screen, where you will then be asked to enter your basic information and contract details.

  • 04. Signature

    In the signature screen you need to sign your name and the name of the other person, you can send the link to the other person to complete the signature.

  • 05. Upload the Contract

    Once the contract has been generated, you will be taken to the IPFS interface where your contract will be automatically saved to our database and you can choose to upload it to IPFS (warning: uploading to IPFS risks exposing the contract and you will no longer be able to modify and delete it).

  • 06. Save the hash value

    Save your hash value and we will save your hash to the BSV blockchain network
