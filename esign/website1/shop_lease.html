 6.1 If Party B pays the deposit overdue, Party A shall have the right to terminate this agreement and request Party B to pay a one-time compensation of GBP 1,000 for the liquidated damages .

6.4 Party B shall not refuse to sign the "House Rental Contract" and its annexes, and "Property Service Annexes" (including the "Provisional Owners Agreement") on the grounds that the terms of the contract cannot be agreed. The deposit paid by Party B is not refundable.
6.5 If required by Party A's overall business planning, Party A has the right to adjust the business format and store number reserved by Party B, which is not a breach of contract by Party A. If Party B disagrees with the adjustment, Party A has the right to terminate this contract and refund the deposit paid by Party B without interest.
6.6 Party B promises that at the time of signing this contract, it has fully understood the processing conditions, procedures and all the fees that need to be paid for the commercial housing, and promises that if it is unable to perform the contract due to its own reasons, it is a breach of contract.
6.7 The parties confirm that they will no longer bear other responsibilities except for the deposit responsibilities as stipulated in this contract.

Article 7. Force Majeure
7.1 Definition of force majeure: refers to all events that occur after the signing of this contract, cannot be foreseen when this contract is signed, whose occurrence and consequences cannot be avoided or overcome, and prevent any party from performing all or part of the contract. The aforementioned events include earthquakes, typhoons, floods, fires, wars, international or domestic transportation interruptions, epidemics, strikes, and other events that are considered force majeure under English laws or general international business practices. One party's lack of funds must be a force majeure event.
7.2 Before the signing of the "House Lease Contract" by both parties, in the event of the aforementioned force majeure, Party A has the right to terminate this fixed-lease contract. If Party A terminates this fixed-lease contract, it shall return the deposit paid by Party B interest-free within working days of the cancellation of this fixed-lease contract , and shall no longer bear other responsibilities.
